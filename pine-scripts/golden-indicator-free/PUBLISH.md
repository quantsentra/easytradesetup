## Long description (the body)

```
WHAT IT DOES

A clean, no-repaint key-levels layer for any market and timeframe:

• CPR (Central Pivot Range) — the prior session compressed into a pivot
  plus a top/bottom central band. Price above the band = bullish bias,
  below = bearish, inside = balance/chop. A narrow band hints a trending
  day may follow; a wide band hints a rangebound day.

• Key levels — prior-day high/low (PDH/PDL) and prior-week high/low
  (PWH/PWL). The reaction magnets where stops cluster and moves pause.

• Lifeline — one trend read (a McGinley Dynamic). Holding above is
  constructive, below is defensive. Context, not an entry trigger.

• Info panel — CPR bias, day-type hint, trend, and CPR width at a glance.

Everything is bar-close and no-repaint: the higher-timeframe levels are
pulled from the previous completed session, so nothing redraws on you
intrabar. Theme-aware — reads cleanly on light and dark charts.

HOW TRADERS USE IT

Mark your bias from where price sits versus the CPR band, watch the
prior-day / prior-week levels for reactions, and use the Lifeline as a
trend filter. It tells you where the important prices are; you decide
what to do with them.

compression), market-structure breaks (BOS / CHoCH), reactive supply &
demand zones, a sealed signal model, eight documented setups with
explicit triggers, stops and exits, alerts, and daily multi-market notes.
```
