## Negative keywords (do not target)

- Anything containing "guaranteed profit", "100% accuracy", "no loss" — brand poison.
- "free signals" — wrong audience.
- "stock tips telegram" — wrong audience.
- Specific stock names + "buy now" — SEBI exposure.
