# Brand Guardian Skill

## When to use
- Writing or reviewing landing page copy
- Drafting email sequences (welcome, follow-up, upsell)
- Writing social media posts (Twitter/X, Telegram, LinkedIn)
- Reviewing any text before it goes in front of customers
- Ensuring SEBI disclaimer compliance

## EasyTradeSetup brand identity

### Personality
- Confident — clear, direct, no hedging
- Practical — real examples, actionable, no fluff
- Accessible — beginner-friendly without being condescending
- Premium — clean, precise, not salesy

### Voice: Do vs Don't

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| "3 clear signals. No guesswork." | "Amazing strategy that always works!" |
| "Enter at candle close." | "You might want to consider entering..." |
| "Stop trading after 2 losses." | "Just trust the system!" |
| "Rs 999 — yours forever." | "Cheap! Limited time offer!" |
| "For Nifty & BankNifty traders." | "Works on any market!" |

### Target audience
- Indian retail F&O trader, 22-45 years old
- Trades BankNifty/Nifty part-time or full-time
- Has lost money, skeptical of "gurus"
- Wants rules, not theory
- Responds to: clear proof, simple rules, honest risk disclosure

## Mandatory disclaimer (use exact text, always)

> Trading in F&O instruments involves significant financial risk. Past performance of any strategy is not indicative of future results. EasyTradeSetup products are for educational purposes only and do not constitute SEBI-registered investment advice. Trade only with capital you can afford to lose.

Never paraphrase. Never shorten. Include on every product page and email.

## Landing page rules

### Headlines
- Lead with outcome or pain, not features
- Max 8 words for H1
- Good: "Stop Guessing. Start Trading With a System."
- Bad: "EasyTradeSetup Intraday Momentum Pack v1.0"

### CTAs
- Primary: "Get the Pack for Rs 999 →"
- Secondary: "See Pricing →"
- Never: "Buy Now", "Click Here", "Subscribe"

### Pricing
- Show all 3 tiers together
- Mark Pro as "Most Popular"
- Show price in Rs (not USD)
- "One-time payment. Instant delivery. No subscription ever."

## Email sequence

### Email 1 — Welcome (immediate)
Subject: "Your EasyTradeSetup pack is ready"
Content: Download link, install steps, what to read first. No upsell.

### Email 2 — Check-in (Day 3)
Subject: "Have you set it up yet?"
Content: Common first mistakes, quick tip, invite reply.

### Email 3 — Upsell (Day 7, Basic buyers only)
Subject: "The next setup Basic buyers miss"
Content: Preview of Pro strategy, upgrade offer. Value-led, not pushy.

## Content review checklist

- [ ] Disclaimer included (for any performance claims)
- [ ] No guaranteed returns language
- [ ] Price accurate and in Rs
- [ ] CTA clear and specific
- [ ] Mobile-readable (short paragraphs, bullets)
- [ ] Passes brand voice check (confident, practical, no hype)

## Output format

1. Reviewed/written content (final copy, not suggestions)
2. Compliance flags (if disclaimer missing or language risky)
3. One-line brand voice note ("Tone correct" or specific fix)
